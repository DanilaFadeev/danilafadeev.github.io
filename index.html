How to work with items

  • Create new item

    • There are two ways to create a new item:

    1. On the top panel choose "Files" > "Create New"

    2. Right click on the desktop area and choose "Create"

  • Delete item

    • For deleting the item lets make a right click on it and choose "Delete"

  • Copy, Cut and Paste

    • This operation is the same with the last one. You can also paste items by Ctrl + V

  • Moving

    • For move the item you have to press left mouse button on it and move it as you want. At the end of the moving you should just unpress left mouse button.
